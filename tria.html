Answers to frequently asked questions

How does the TRIA card differ from the ANOR installment card?

TRIA card is a payment card, which can be used to store your savings. Moreover, you can withdraw cash, receive cashback on purchases in our partner network, receive interest on the balance of the card and incur debt, if there is not enough money.

What documents do I need to apply for a bank card?

Document of identification is sufficient.

How can I top my card up?

You can top your account up at ATMs of other banks or transfer money from card to card.

How can I know my card balance?

The card balance is always visible in the ANORBANK mobile application or online banking. You can also call the Contact Center.

How can I get report on the card?

You can get the report in the ANORBANK mobile application or online banking.

Can a relative or friend get a card for me?

Sadly no. We deliver the card only personally to the clients of ANORBANK for the safety of your funds and data.

I've lost (broke, contactless payment stopped working) my card. What should I do?

Freeze your card in the ANORBANK mobile application, online banking, or call the Contact Center.

How can I block/unblock my card?

You can always do it in the ANORBANK mobile application, online banking, or call the Contact Center.

How can I find by card's details?

You always have access to your account and card details in the ANORBANK mobile application or online banking.

What is an overdraft?

Overdraft is an opportunity to borrow small amounts from a bank for a short period. If you have an overdraft enabled, you can spend more money than you have in your account.
